# Local Business Digitizer — `/bq-local-business` (C13) — alpha.24

Turn an offline/low-tech local business into a **practical minimum digital system** — not an enterprise build. Command: `bq-local-business.md`. Skills: automation-engineer + product-strategist + localization-rtl + make-money. Reuses C6/C11/C12 rather than duplicating them.

## Why a command

Distinct recurring MENA-SMB workflow with its own 10-artifact output set and local context (WhatsApp-first, cash-vs-online, phone workflows, low-tech staff, Arabic/RTL) that doesn't fit cleanly inside offer/automation alone. High productization value (one workflow, many SMB clients).

## Outputs — `.bequite/local-business/`

BUSINESS_PROFILE · PAIN_MAP · DIGITAL_OPPORTUNITY_MAP · MINIMUM_DIGITAL_SYSTEM · AUTOMATION_IDEAS · LOCAL_MARKET_NOTES · IMPLEMENTATION_ROADMAP · RISK_AND_COMPLIANCE · OFFER_ANGLE · NEXT_STEPS.

## Principle

**Minimum viable digital system first** — usually a simple menu/catalog page + WhatsApp ordering + booking/reminders, NOT a full custom app. Phased roadmap. Local-market realism over tech maximalism. RISK_AND_COMPLIANCE covers data/privacy (PDPL/MENA where relevant). OFFER_ANGLE feeds C11 so the digitizer becomes a sellable service. **Built alpha.24 — NOT live-tested.**
