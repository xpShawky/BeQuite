# Brand Kit Engine — `/bq-brand-kit` (C14) — alpha.24

Build a **non-generic** brand identity from niche + audience + competitors + positioning. Command: `bq-brand-kit.md`. Skills: ux-ui-designer + frontend-design-system + researcher + writing-dna + localization-rtl. Reference-safe (shares C3's originality discipline).

## Why a command

Distinct creative workflow, 13-artifact set, research-driven, recurring. Anti-generic is the whole point — a thin argument couldn't carry the niche-fit reasoning.

## Outputs — `.bequite/brand/`

BRAND_BRIEF · AUDIENCE_AND_POSITIONING · COMPETITOR_AND_REFERENCE_SCAN · NAMING_DIRECTIONS · BRAND_DNA · VISUAL_IDENTITY · COLOR_STRATEGY · TYPOGRAPHY_STRATEGY · LOGO_BRIEF · DO_AND_DONT_ASSOCIATIONS · CONTENT_TONE · ASSET_ROADMAP · NEXT_STEPS.

## Anti-generic discipline

Every color/type choice carries **why-it-fits-this-niche** reasoning (no default Inter + purple-blue gradient); naming gives several directions with rationale, not one bland name; a competitor/reference scan finds the sameness gap and the brand differentiates from it; reference-safe (patterns not pixels, no cloning); platform fit (YouTube/LinkedIn/X/FB/IG/website/packaging). LOGO_BRIEF is a brief for a designer/tool — BeQuite specs identity, does not fabricate a final logo image. **Built alpha.24 — NOT live-tested.**
