# Application Tracker

User-maintained tracker for jobs applied to. BeQuite populates the template; user updates status as applications progress.

---

| Date | Platform / Company | Role / Task | Status | Pitch used | Follow-up date | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| <date> | <name> | <role> | applied / interview / offer / rejected / no-reply | <pitch template name> | <date> | <notes> |

---

## Status legend

- **applied** — sent, awaiting response
- **interview** — got past initial filter
- **offer** — received offer; pending accept/reject
- **accepted** — accepted offer; track payout on first paycheck
- **rejected** — got a no
- **no-reply** — > 14 days, treat as no
- **ghosted** — got partial reply, then nothing

---

## Tips

- Set follow-up date 7-14 days after applying
- Re-apply at the same platform with a different role only after 30 days
- Track pitch effectiveness — note which template got the most responses
- Update `PITCH_TEMPLATES.md` based on what works
